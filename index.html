About MurphsLife & Casa Conejo

We're not planning this from a conference room.

MurphsLife Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit building regenerative communities, humanitarian programs, and mission-aligned social enterprises across Latin America.

Casa Conejo is our flagship campus in El Salvador — a 46-acre regenerative property being developed as an eco-resort, working farm, training center, and community hub. It's part of a broader national regenerative development portfolio in partnership with the Salvadoran government.

We're not theorizing. We're building. The summit will happen on land we're actively stewarding — which means the context is real, the teachers will be real, and the outcomes will be measurable.
